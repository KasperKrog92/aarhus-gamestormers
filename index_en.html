About Aarhus Gamestormers

Aarhus Gamestormers is a monthly video game club for adults in Aarhus. Like a book club, we meet to discuss a selected game that everyone has played ahead of the meeting. Our aim is to explore games as an art form and interactive storytelling, while building a welcoming social space for people who love game and nerd culture.

The meetings are informal and friendly — there’s no requirement to have finished the game, but expect spoilers. Our hub is on Discord, where communication is in English. Everyone is welcome, including those who can’t make it to the in-person meetings. You must be at least 18 years old to join.
