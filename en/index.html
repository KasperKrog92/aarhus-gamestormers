About Aarhus Gamestormers

Aarhus Gamestormers is a monthly video game club for adults in Aarhus. Like a book club, we meet to discuss a chosen game that everyone has played in the weeks leading up to the meetup.

The idea is to take games seriously as an art form and a medium for storytelling, while also just having a good time with people who care about the same things.

Meetups are informal. You don't have to finish the game, but expect spoilers. The club runs on Discord and communication is in English. Everyone is welcome, including those who can't make it in person. You need to be at least 18 to join.
